Spacing

Spacing system in leaf creates an incremental spacing system pre-determined spacing gaps.

_spacing.scss

Formula: .[m|p|mx|my|px|py|mt|mr|mb|ml|pt|pr|pb|pl]-[100|200|300|400|500|600|700|800|800|900]

Size guide

We use the following guide for referring to the space incrementing. You can append any of the sizing classes (listed in below tables) with these sizes to create spaces accordingly:


size post-fix increment size
100 4px
200 8px
300 16px
400 24px
500 32px
600 48px
700 64px
800 88px
900 128px

Class Name guide

We use the following class names for creating spacing (margins and paddings) with items:


classname effect
.m-{size} Applies margins on all sides of specified size.
.p-{size} Applies padding on all sides of specified size.
.mx-{size} Applies margins on top and bottom sides of specified size.
.px-{size} Applies padding on top and bottom sides of specified size.
.my-{size} Applies margins on left and right sides of specified size.
.py-{size} Applies padding on left and right sides of specified size.
.mt-{size} Applies margin on top side of specified size.
.mr-{size} Applies margin on right side of specified size.
.mb-{size} Applies margin on bottom side of specified size.
.ml-{size} Applies margin on left side of specified size.
.pt-{size} Applies padding on top side of specified size.
.pr-{size} Applies padding on right side of specified size.
.pb-{size} Applies padding on bottom side of specified size.
.pl-{size} Applies padding on left side of specified size.
