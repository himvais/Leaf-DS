Spacing

Spacing system in leaf creates an incremental spacing system pre-determined spacing gaps.

_spacing.scss

Formula: .[m|p|mx|my|px|py|mt|mr|mb|ml|pt|pr|pb|pl]-[100|200|300|400|500|600|700|800|800|900]

Class Name guide

We use the following class names for creating spacing (margins and paddings) with items:


classname effect
.m-{size}

Applies margins on all sides of specified size.
.p-{size}

Applies padding on all sides of specified size.
.mx-{size}

Applies margins on top and bottom sides of specified size.
.px-{size}

Applies padding on top and bottom sides of specified size.
.my-{size}

Applies margins on left and right sides of specified size.
.py-{size}

Applies padding on left and right sides of specified size.
.mt-{size}

Applies margin on top side of specified size.
.mr-{size}

Applies margin on right side of specified size.
.mb-{size}

Applies margin on bottom side of specified size.
.ml-{size}

Applies margin on left side of specified size.
.pt-{size}

Applies padding on top side of specified size.
.pr-{size}

Applies padding on right side of specified size.
.pb-{size}

Applies padding on bottom side of specified size.
.pl-{size}

Applies padding on left side of specified size.
